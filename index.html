Small games with a softer pace

Games for Lili is an indie studio for browser games that are simple to open, easy to understand, and gentle enough for repeated play. The focus is on healthy game loops: curiosity, practice, imagination, and little moments of progress without pressure.

No ads, no tracking, no dark patterns

  • No behavioral advertising or third-party analytics scripts.
  • No manipulative timers, loot boxes, or attention traps.
  • No accounts required just to play a small browser game.
  • No loud clutter where a calm game would do better.
